Learn to Play Blackjack

  • Deck of 52 cards are shuffled at the start of the game.
  • The hands with the highest point value (not exceeding 21), will win the round.
  • Total value of more than 21 = bust. If player busts, he/she loses, even if dealer also busts -> Blackjack favours the dealer.
  • You may change your wager after each round.
